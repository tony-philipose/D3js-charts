JOHN LEGEND

ABOUT

John Legend was born on December 28, 1978, in Springfield, Ohio. He became an in-demand session musician and songwriter, working with such artists as Alicia Keys, Twista and Janet Jackson, before collaborating with up-and-coming hip-hop artist Kanye West.
